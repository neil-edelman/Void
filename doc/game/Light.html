Lighting effects! Unfortunately, UBOs, which allow structs, were introduced in GLSL3.1; using 1.1 for compatibility, and only have 1.2. Ergo, this is a little bit messy. Maybe in 10 years I'll do a change. <p> Lights depend on external unsigned integers to keep track. Don't modify the light with a function not in this and don't free the unsigned without calling Light_. <p> Fixme: order the lights by distance/brighness and take the head of the list, and ignore all that pass a certain threshold. This will scale way better.
